Image map

It work on an image

If we are going to just embed the generated mosaic, it should just be an image. The below will work.

In the image tag (or svg) usemap="mosaic"

  <img src="../../src/client/assets/baneCat.jpg" alt="Bane Cat" usemap="mosaic">
  <map name="mosaic">
    <area shape="rect" coords="0,0,20,20" href="http://www.google.com" alt="Google">
    <area shape="rect" coords="20,0,40,20" href="http://www.bbc.com" alt="BBC">
  </map>
